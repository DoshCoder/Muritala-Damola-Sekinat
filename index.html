I am a dedicated forestry research professional with a Master's degree in Forest Ecology and Conservation. Currently serving as a Research Fellow II at the Forestry Research Institute of Nigeria (FRIN), I specialize in community ecology, forest conservation, forest ecology, and medicinal plants research.

Professional Focus

My research work centers on sustainable forest management, biodiversity conservation, and the integration of traditional ecological knowledge with modern scientific approaches to address environmental challenges. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Forestry.

With extensive fieldwork experience in Nigeria's diverse ecosystems, I have contributed to multiple research projects and publications in both national and international journals. My work aims to bridge the gap between scientific research and practical forest conservation strategies.
